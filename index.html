What's the difference between an 'Inadmissible' and 'Rejected' visa?

This is a crucial distinction. An **Inadmissible** visa means the authority requires more information or a document correction; it is not a final denial. In this case, our service includes resubmitting the application as many times as needed at no extra charge for our legal fees. A **Rejected** visa is a final, discretionary decision. In this case, our service is considered complete, and reapplying would require a new service agreement after a 6-month waiting period.

How long does the entire process take?

Once all documents are gathered and the application is submitted, the review period by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs is typically 3 to 5 weeks. Document collection can add additional time to this timeline.
